Grammar Notes

1. Bài học trọng tâm

Nhấn vào từng thẻ để xem giải thích, công thức và ví dụ.

Gradable adjectives là tính từ có thể tăng/giảm mức độ: hơi, khá, rất, cực kỳ.

a bit / quite / very / really / extremely + adjective

very cold = rất lạnh

quite expensive = khá đắt

a little tired = hơi mệt

really interesting = thật sự thú vị

Non-gradable adjectives là tính từ đã mang nghĩa rất mạnh hoặc gần như tuyệt đối.

absolutely / completely / totally + strong adjective
Không nên dùngNên dùngNghĩa
very freezingabsolutely freezinglạnh cóng
very exhaustedcompletely exhaustedkiệt sức
very perfectabsolutely perfecthoàn hảo
very tinyabsolutely tinycực nhỏ

Dùng để so sánh 2 người/vật/nơi/ý tưởng.

A + be + adjective-er / more adjective + than + B
  • short → shorter: My bag is smaller than yours.
  • easy → easier: This test is easier than the last one.
  • interesting → more interesting: The book is more interesting than the film.
  • good → better, bad → worse: This café is better than that one.

Dùng để nói một người/vật là nhất trong một nhóm.

the + adjective-est / the most adjective
  • Nam is the tallest student in my class.
  • This is the most difficult question.
  • It is one of the most beautiful places in my country.

as...as dùng để nói hai thứ ngang bằng. not as...as dùng để nói không bằng.

A + be + as + adjective + as + B
A + be + not as + adjective + as + B

My phone is as expensive as yours.

This film is not as exciting as the book.

French is not as easy as English.

My school is bigger than yours = Your school is not as big as mine.

2. Mẫu câu dùng trong PET Speaking & Writing

Mô tả tranh

The first picture looks more crowded than the second one.

Đưa ý kiến

I think watching a film is more relaxing than going shopping.

For me, English is easier than Maths.

This is one of the best choices for students.

Viết email PET

Hi Anna, I think the cinema is better than the shopping centre because it is more relaxing. It is not as noisy as the mall, and the tickets are cheaper than before.

4. Luyện ứng dụng vào Speaking & Writing

Speaking Prompt

Task: Compare two places for a school trip: a museum and a theme park.

I think the theme park is more exciting than the museum, but it is not as educational as the museum. The museum is quieter and cheaper, so it may be better for younger students.

Writing Prompt

Task: Write an email to your friend. Compare two activities for the weekend: watching a film or going to a café.

Hi Sam, I think watching a film is better than going to a café because it is more interesting. The new cinema is not as expensive as the old one, and it is one of the most comfortable places in town. See you soon!
